Legend of Colors

Each author node is colored based on the cluster in which the majority of their texts fall into. For this time period, k-means clustering with manual feature engineering and TFIDF vectorization yields four clusters (Clusters 0 to 3). Each edge is colored based on the manual categorization of different n-grams. Node colors are encoded as circles, while edge colors are represented in boxes.
